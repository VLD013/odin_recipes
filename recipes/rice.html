How to cook Rice

  1. Clean the rice with some wather
  2. Boil 2 cups of wather in a covered pot
  3. When it start to boil, add 1/4 teaspoon of salt and 1 cup of rice
  4. Cover the pot for around 20 min
  5. Once the rice looks as fluffy as can be, take it off the burner and let sit for 5 minutes
