How to boil pasta

  1. Put some wather to boil in a cauldron
    2. Add some salt
  2. When boiling the wather add the quantity of pasta wanted
  3. Let boil between 10 and 15 minutes
  4. When is ready drain it
  5. Now the pasta is ready to be added in sauce or to be serve in a plate already, as you desire
  6. Enjoy it !
